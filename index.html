05 / Questions, answered

Frequently asked
questions.

What areas do you cover?+

Angus is North London based. Please get in touch with your postcode to discuss your location.

Do you specialise in a particular type of piano?+

Yes — the service specialises in upright pianos.

What is a pitch raise?+

A pitch raise is for a piano that has fallen substantially below its intended pitch. It involves a larger initial correction before a fine tuning, so it is priced separately at £190.

How do I know which service I need?+

If you are unsure, share what you know about the piano and when it was last tuned. Angus can help you decide whether a standard tuning or pitch raise is likely to be appropriate.

How do I book a piano tuning?+

Call 07803 306679 or email angusvardywhite@gmail.com with your location and a little information about your piano.
